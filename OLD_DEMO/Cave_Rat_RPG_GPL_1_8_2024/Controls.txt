W: Forward

Right Click: Faster Attack (Consumes more Mana - Blue Bar)

Numpad 8: Mute music
Numpad 8: Unmute music ((ノಠ益ಠ)ノhit it again! reeeee)

+ : Increase music volume
- : Decrease music volume
